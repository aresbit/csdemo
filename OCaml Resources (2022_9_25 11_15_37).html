OCaml Resources for the Paradigm Conference

Foreword

Calling all adolescent Ocamlers (and Ocamlers to-be)! This document is a list of articles and videos that we found super helpful when we began to learn OCaml (as well as some that we created ourselves).  They are grouped by topic, and we’ve annotated each of these with a color to indicate their difficulty level (green → easy, yellow → medium, red → hard). Furthermore, each resource has an annotation indicating what type of source it is (a → article/website/chapter, v → video, p → practice problem), as well as some other information (* → we recommend,  ** → we made it, *** → contact us for access).  PLEASE DO NOT REDISTRIBUTE PAID MATERIALS!

Finally, this is a living, breathing document; we are language-learners too! If you notice any mistakes, have ideas for things you’d like to add, or need any help in general, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at hello@esolangconf.com. Happy coding!
